Optional Saturday Excursion

If you're up for it, we're planning an optional excursion (field trip!) the day after the wedding. We'll be covering transportation. If you're interested please let us know in the RSVP form! Here are the details:

  • Uxmal: Uxmal is an ancient Maya city of the classical period located in present-day Mexico. It is considered one of the most important archaeological sites of Maya culture.
  • Cenote Dzombacal: Swim in the Yucatan's famous natural sinkholes. Dzombacal is a semi-open cenote with crystal clear water. It's an ideal place for swimming, bird watching, and diving.
